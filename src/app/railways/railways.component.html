Electrical engineers in the Indian Railways play a vital role in keeping the trains running smoothly and safely. They are part of the prestigious Indian Railway Service of Electrical Engineers (IRSEE)

Deals with the operation and maintenance of electric locomotives. Their tasks involve monitoring power consumption, ensuring crew safety and adherence to protocols, and analyzing locomotive performance data to identify potential problems. They may also be involved in coordinating with workshops for repairs and scheduling maintenance activities.
